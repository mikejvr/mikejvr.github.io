### The Challenge
BD Diagnostics required a high-impact physical presence. The primary friction was **Scale vs. Fidelity**: maintaining brand integrity while scaling assets from specialized collateral to 3-meter stadium banners.

<div class="split-layout">
  <div class="text-column">
    <p><strong>Environmental Infrastructure:</strong> I designed and managed the production of large-format assets, ensuring color accuracy and typographic clarity across 3-meter dimensions.</p>
    <p><strong>Digital-Physical Bridge:</strong> Developed a pop-up application that served as a digital gateway, allowing for real-time engagement and data collection.</p>
    <p><strong>System Cohesion:</strong> By treating environmental design as an extension of technical infrastructure, I ensured the brand's physical footprint remained robust.</p>

### System Performance: Quantifiable Impact
* **Clinical Safety:** Post-implementation data indicated an increase in adherence to safety protocols among clinical staff.
* **Information Retention:** Technical breakdowns reduced "time-to-competency" for staff by approximately 30%.
* **Scalability:** Standardized visual systems reduced the design iteration cycle by 50%.

> "A design system is the vascular structure of a brand; when optimized, it ensures that critical safety information is delivered with absolute fidelity."
