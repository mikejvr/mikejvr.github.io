### The Challenge
The Ascendant Group is a high-stakes branding and public relations firm. Your work for them is "B2B Professional"—it needs to look authoritative, polished, and trustworthy.

"At The Ascendant Group, I acted as the design partner for a roster of high-impact leaders, authors, and entrepreneurs. My role was to translate their personal visions into cohesive visual ecosystems—whether that meant designing a 200-page manuscript for an NBA veteran or crafting the visual identity for community-driven entrepreneurial initiatives. I focused on building systems that did more than look good; they were designed to command authority, educate audiences, and drive direct engagement for my clients' brands."
